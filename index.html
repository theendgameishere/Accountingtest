Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services.

What should I bring to my tax appointment?

For your individual tax return, please bring your W-2s, 1099s, records of any other income, information on tax-deductible expenses (like mortgage interest, student loan interest, or charitable contributions), and a copy of last year's tax return if you are a new client.

Do you only work during tax season?

No, we are open year-round to help with all your financial needs, including tax planning, bookkeeping, payroll, and any questions you have for the IRS. Our regular business hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

How much do your services cost?

Our fees are based on the complexity of the work involved. We offer a free initial consultation to discuss your needs and provide a transparent quote. Our goal is to provide affordable, high-value service to our community.

Do you specialize in small business accounting?

Yes, absolutely. We have decades of experience serving small businesses in the Millbrook area. We offer a full range of services including business tax preparation, bookkeeping, payroll, and new business advisory.
